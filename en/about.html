About GaKaWa

GaKaWa is dedicated to building stable, approachable, and long-form reading tools for Tibetan texts, helping more people read, study, and preserve Tibetan literature through digital tools.

We believe a good reading tool should help readers focus on the text, not the interface.

Current Product

GaKaWa Tibetan Reader is an app for Tibetan reading, learning, and study.
